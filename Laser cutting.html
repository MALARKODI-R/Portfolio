ASSIGNMENT - 1

Introduction

The objective of this assignment is to design and fabricate a segmented 3D sphere in Fusion 360, extract 2D projections suitable for laser cutting, and finally assemble the segments into a spherical structure. This workflow integrates advanced 3D modeling techniques, precise 2D documentation, and physical fabrication strategies, offering a comprehensive experience from concept to physical model.

Conclusion

This assignment demonstrates the complete pipeline of digital design to physical prototyping. By modeling a complex 3D object like a segmented sphere, creating precise 2D documentation, and fabricating using a laser cutter, learners gain hands-on experience with CAD tools, dimensioning practices, and real-world engineering production methods. This integration of skills prepares students for industrial design, product development, and digital fabrication workflows.
